About me

I am a graduate student in theoretical computer science at UChicago. I am very fortunate to have Prof. Tulsiani as my advisor. I received a B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from Unicamp Brazil (my home country) and also received an engineering degree from Telecom Paris (France) as part of a double degree exchange program (details can be found in my CV).

I have been investigating problems involving coding theory, the sum-of-squares hierarchy, high-dimensional expanders and representation theory of the symmetric group. I am particularly intrigued by the (many) intersection(s) of CS theory and mathematics.
