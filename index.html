About me

I am a graduate student in theoretical computer science at UChicago. I am very fortunate to work with Prof. Tulsiani.

I have been investigating problems involving high-dimensional expanders, the sum-of-squares hierarchy, representation theory of the symmetric group and coding theory. I am particularly intrigued by the (many) intersection(s) of CS theory and mathematics.
